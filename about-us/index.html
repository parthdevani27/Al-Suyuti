About Us

We have done exceptional things in the past. Today, we are doing exceptional things for the future.

Goldmarine Exports has a rich history of raising the bar in the seafood industry. Established in 1997, the focus has always been on export of live & fresh seafood. Through the last two decades, our company has been expanding rapidly with mission to exceed industry standards by offering an extensive product line, focusing on product innovation and quality control. Being an old hand at this, we go by the “nom de plume” of purveyors of seafood in India.

We stand by our mission to deliver quality seafood. We are already one of the biggest exporters of seafood to countries in the Middle East, Far East, South East Asia and South Asia.

Our branches are in 6 locations. However, our purchasing points run throughout the coast of Bay of Bengal, Arabian Sea and the Andaman islands. So, when the demand is more, we are more than equipped to deliver our line of seafood products within a short period of time. We have achieved 100% turnover on a year on year basis and our combined turnover has crossed over 15 million USD.

Local Retail Venture

 

Snapper is our online seafood retail venture. Having beein in the seafood industry for almost two decades, it was natural for us to extend our experience of sourcing, preparing and packaging the best seafood by entering into the local market as a major retail player.  The retail venture is pushed via a seperate brand – SNAPPER.

The major impetus for marketing SNAPPER is via our online portal, www.snapper.co.in. A dedicated team of digital marketing experts caters to the seafood needs of residents within the Chennai city region. We do just about everthing we can to make sure that all our customers are satisfied. The products delivered are fresh, gourmet and in some cases live. We deal wih a variety of seafood from succulent tender shrimp to world class coral trout, grouper, red snapper and more.

Unlike many online seafood companies, we go through with the due diligence we do with our seafood exports with our local deliveries as well. We process, package and deliver our products all from the same location – our company headquarters in Chintadripet, Chennai.
